Phreesia — Senior Product Manager

Healthcare Technology · Current Role

At Phreesia, I'm focused on making care better every day through data. My mandate is to establish a suite of data products that give healthcare practices clear, actionable visibility into how patients are being scheduled, processed, and billed — and to give Phreesia itself the cross-practice intelligence to continuously improve the platform.

A defining thread of this work is enabling data to be AI-usable: structuring, governing, and surfacing information in ways that allow machine intelligence to act on it reliably. This isn't just a technical challenge — it requires deep collaboration with clinical, operational, and business stakeholders to ensure the right questions are being answered in the right way.

Teck Resources — Data Scientist → Product Manager

Mining & Natural Resources · Copper, Zinc, Lead & Steelmaking Coal

I joined Teck as a Data Scientist embedded in mineral processing — the operational bottleneck of any mine site, where incremental improvements translate directly into significant financial returns. I built AI and ML-powered tools that gave operators real-time recommendations to optimize complex physical and chemical processes, and developed simulation tools that helped mine sites anticipate and manage material variability days in advance.

Recognizing the opportunity to have broader strategic impact, I transitioned into a Product Manager role. In that capacity, I led a team of developers, managed a budget and roadmap, and worked across the full organizational hierarchy — from operators on the plant floor to vice-presidents in the boardroom. I also led the development of a real-time digital twin of mine site material flows, a product now being scaled across all Teck sites and joint ventures.

Rocky Mountaineer — Data Scientist

Luxury Travel · Canadian Rockies Rail

My career began at Rocky Mountaineer, where I built data products to optimize sales and marketing across North America. I developed tools that identified at-risk customers, modeled revenue scenarios for annual planning, and powered automated market segmentation down to the individual address level — enabling the marketing team to target with precision at scale.

I also built the analytical infrastructure that gave senior leadership a real-time view of all revenue flows, turning what had been a fragmented reporting landscape into a single, trusted source of truth for decision-making. This role grounded me in the full cycle of product development: from understanding a business problem, through building and validating a solution, to driving adoption and measuring impact.
