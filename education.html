Education

Engineering roots, a business pivot,
and a graduate specialization in data science.

Master of Data Science

A rigorous graduate specialization that brought together business acumen, computer science, and statistics. This program was the foundation for every data product and AI system I've built since — bridging the gap between raw analytical capability and real-world application.

Bachelor of Commerce

Operations Management & Computer Science

I started university in engineering, drawn by its emphasis on mathematics and problem-solving. But I wanted to understand how those methods applied at the scale of organizations and markets — so I transitioned into Commerce, specializing in Operations Management and Computer Science. Operations Management encompasses the planning, organizing, and oversight of business processes to enhance efficiency and deliver value. Combining it with computer science gave me a unique lens I've carried throughout my career.

Beyond the Classroom

Throughout university, I held volunteer roles in student, sports, and charity organizations — generally leading Finance and Operations functions. Those experiences shaped how I think about teams, accountability, and getting things done. Leading in those environments — where there's no authority to fall back on, only influence and trust — remains some of the most formative work I've done.
