About Me

Entrepreneur, builder, and systems thinker.
Motivated to create something new and make an impact.

How I Work

I'm an entrepreneur through and through — a problem-solver and a builder. I observe problems and see them as opportunities, and I'm motivated to create something new and make an impact. Comfortable with uncertainty, I acknowledge, analyze, mitigate, and accept risk as part of the process.

I thrive on the full product lifecycle: finding a want or a need, ideating, prototyping, building, launching, and scaling solutions — all while reflecting on what I've learned and adjusting course. I'm naturally curious and will dive deep into the details of a problem to understand what's truly at the root of it. I'm naturally tech-savvy and love rolling up my sleeves to understand the deep context of a solution — but I don't get stuck in the weeds.

I'm a systems thinker and a data-driven decision-maker. I don't get distracted and have a clear vision of what needs to get done, when, and why. I balance trade-offs and ensure strategy is executed against — always thinking about how decisions made today affect where we'll be in 6, 12, 18, and 24 months.

I work best surrounded by teams that strive for excellence. I believe teams are who get things done — through combined skills and competencies, shared ownership and drive, and multiple viewpoints. I gravitate toward leadership roles and others look to me for direction, guidance, coaching, and mentorship. I'm level-headed, humble, and empathetic.

Outside of Work

I'm a naturally optimistic person — I always look on the bright side of life, and my passion tends to be contagious to the people around me. I'm active in sports and outdoor activities: soccer, running, biking, swimming, hiking, camping, and rock-climbing. I keep up with sports, though I'd much rather play than watch.

I value time with my wife and newborn daughter and love sharing my interests with them. I have a great love of food and will always seek out new and memorable experiences. I love to travel, immersing myself in local culture and history. And I'll happily spend an evening around a table with friends and a good board game.

Outside of the office, I'm also a tech enthusiast who keeps up with the cutting edge — currently focused on AI, both in workflows and in agents. I weave those learnings into my day-to-day, continuously improving how I work.

Where I'm Headed

My goal is to build better tech and use tech to better business — ultimately to better the human experience. AI is an evolution in the tech landscape, much like Web 2.0 was. I want to be at the forefront of organizations harnessing the potential of AI to 10x their outcomes. There are countless wants and needs that can be addressed with AI-powered tools, and the technology is now there to build upon.
